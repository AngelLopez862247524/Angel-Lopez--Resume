Civic Engagement

As you can see on my resume, I have a strong background in office products, community engagement, being a team player, and being an effective communicator. My role as a peer Advisor/Student assistant was to assist the program coordinator of the Hispanic Serving Institute- El Centro with office procedures like data entry, requisitions, budget transfers, hiring (faculty, outreach specialists, student assistants, adjunct counselors), managing the calendar with important dates, and follow-ups. My role as a proactive team player has had great success in working with staff and developing cultural and educational programs that serve low-income students. These initiatives have allowed me to develop and refine my training and organization skills, and it has become evident that assisting and supporting others has been a great passion of mine.

I continue to be a proactive leader in the C.A.R.N.A.L. support program, whose mission is to provide positive Latino role models in our community. Sharing personal experiences and professionalism with our local community can continue to have a positive impact. For this reason, I strongly believe that having the opportunity to portray my drive towards assisting others and the community and to engage in and access quality services can benefit any team.
